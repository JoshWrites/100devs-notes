"What would udo if LinkedIn forced u to upload ID? There are many articles this week with Discord and LinkedIn and all the Persona data leaks. Can I avoid LI?"
Leon: Not having a linkedin is closing a very very big door. it's a big part of a lot of peoples professional lives. they only want you to have one account, and they want to make sure it's real people on there. especially if you come from a non-US area... it depends on your area, but it's a very important door to have open. it's unfortunate, but it's coming. a lot of platforms are doing this, rolling it back, and trying to make it more palatable.
